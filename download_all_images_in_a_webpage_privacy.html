Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Image Grabber Chrome Extension handles user data. Our primary goal is to provide a powerful tool while maintaining 100% user privacy.

The Short Version: Image Grabber does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or website content to any external servers. All operations happen locally on your computer.

1. Data Collection

Image Grabber does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not track your name, email address, location, or IP address. We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.

2. Use of Permissions

To function correctly, the extension requires specific technical permissions:

  • activeTab: Used only to identify image URLs on the page you explicitly choose to scan.
  • scripting: Used to detect images within the website's document structure (DOM).
  • downloads: Used solely to save the selected images to your local "Downloads" folder.

3. Data Transmission

No data is ever sent to the developer or any third party. The image URLs are processed locally within your browser and passed directly to the Chrome Download Manager.

4. Third-Party Websites

While using Image Grabber, you may download content from various third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date."

6. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this policy, you may contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support dashboard.
